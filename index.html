Meet Angie Mays

Angie Mays is an empathetic and empowering mindset coach who specializes in helping women rediscover their inner spark and reclaim their lives. After her own transformative journey, Angie learned how to manage her mental health after 20 years of searching for solutions.

She understands firsthand the challenges that women face when feeling lost, disoriented, or disconnected from their true selves. Through years of trial, self-reflection, and growth, Angie found the tools and practices that helped her regain control and stability, and now she's dedicated to sharing these with others.

CEPC Accredited Master Coach

Certified by the International Authority for Professional Coaching & Mentoring. Combining training in cognitive behavioural therapy and mindfulness with wisdom gained from personal experience to guide women through healing and empowerment.

Professional Certifications

With Angie's support, women leave not just with newfound clarity, but with a deep sense of empowerment and an unwavering love for themselves and their life.
